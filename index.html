What is D4SG?

The D4SG (Data for Social Good) Hackathon aims to promote social good through technology and computer science, and to drive sustained innovation and crowd-source solutions to address pressing real-life business problems and social issues.

We are bringing together +100 passionate and innovative technologists to build software solutions that address specific challenges facing industry today. The panel of subject matter experts will ensure an accurate, fair and transparent process.

About DSAi

The Data Science and AI Association of Australia is a non-for-profit association determined to connect the data scientist and machine learning community of Australia seeking to gain and share knowledge, grow their network and collaborate on projects.

Since its inception in 2019, DSAi has committed to hosting and organising a range of activities like meetups, workshops, study groups and publications, aimed to helping professionals understand industry trends, employment scenarios and stay on top of the latest research.
